OP: Broke and tired of the meal plan. Where's the cheap eats near UTA?

comment:
The strip along Cooper St and UTA Blvd is the move. Tons of fast food and taco spots within
walking distance, most stuff under $10. The taquerias are open late and huge portions.

comment:
The Mav Express markets on campus take meal-plan dining dollars and have grab-and-go stuff if
you don't want a full sit-down. Cheaper than ordering delivery every night.

comment:
Pho and ramen places just off campus are clutch — one big bowl is like two meals if you save
half. Student favorite for a reason.

comment:
Pro tip: a lot of the local spots do student discounts if you show your Mav ID. Always ask,
worst case they say no.

comment:
If you have a car or split a rideshare, the international grocery stores nearby are stupid
cheap for cooking your own food. Buying a rice cooker freshman year paid for itself.

comment:
Free food is the real cheat code. Follow the campus orgs on social — there's a club event with
free pizza basically every week if you pay attention.
